Marksmanship Hunter

Marksmanship Hunters in World of Warcraft are adept at dealing massive damage to single targets with long-range attacks using ranged weapons. Their playstyle involves careful positioning, precise targeting, and tactical decision-making, along with abilities such as traps, crowd control, and damage-boosting buffs.

Beastmaster Hunter

Beastmaster Hunters in World of Warcraft use a pet to fight alongside them, customizing its abilities and appearance. Their playstyle involves balancing ranged attacks with their pet's melee attacks and using abilities to control and enhance their pet's performance, relying on their bond with their pet to take down enemies.

Elemental Shaman

Elemental Shamans in World of Warcraft cast spells that deal elemental damage, using abilities for crowd control and buffs. Their playstyle involves managing resources for maximum damage output and providing support with healing and utility spells. They are versatile spellcasters who bring a mix of damage and support to any group.

Balance Druid

Balance Druids in World of Warcraft cast spells that deal nature and arcane damage using abilities for crowd control and healing. Their playstyle involves managing resources for maximum damage output while providing support with healing and utility spells. They are versatile spellcasters who bring a mix of damage and support to any group.

Shadow Priest

Shadow Priests in World of Warcraft cast shadow spells and use abilities for crowd control and healing. Their playstyle involves resource management for damage output and support, making them versatile spellcasters who are more effective against certain types of enemies.

Devastion Evoker

Devastation evokers are a new class in World of Warcraft that use hard-casted abilities and AoE damage to take out large groups of enemies. They use a variety of instant cast spells to fill in the gaps in their destruction dealing.

Fire/Frost/Arcane mage

Mages in World of Warcraft cast spells that deal different types of damage using abilities for crowd control and utility. Their playstyle involves resource management for maximum damage output while providing support with crowd control and utility spells. Mages have a high skill ceiling and are versatile spellcasters who bring a mix of damage and support to any group.

Destruction/Affliction/Demonology Warlock

Warlocks in World of Warcraft cast spells that deal shadow and fire damage, while draining the life force of their enemies. Their playstyle involves resource management for maximum damage output and support with crowd control and utility spells. Warlocks can also summon demons to fight alongside them, making them powerful and versatile spellcasters in combat.
