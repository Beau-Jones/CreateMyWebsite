Choosing a Main

In World of Warcraft, you can choose between 13 classes to play. The character you devote the most time to is called a main.
The classes in World of Warcraft are as follows:

  • Warrior
  • Paladin
  • Death Knight
  • Hunter
  • Shaman
  • Druid
  • Rogue
  • Monk
  • Demon Hunter
  • Mage
  • Priest
  • Warlock
  • Evoker
